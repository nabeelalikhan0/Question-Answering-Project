About Our Project

Welcome to our advanced AI-powered Chatbot platform that integrates file upload and intelligent conversational capabilities to assist users with contextual answers based on their uploaded documents. Our goal is to provide a smart, efficient, and user-friendly assistant that helps users interact naturally, enabling productive workflows and enhanced accessibility to information.
